Introduction

Welcome to this style guide, where you’ll find a concise overview of our design principles and visual standards. Whether you’re creating new content or updating existing elements, these guidelines will ensure consistency and clarity across our one-pager.

Typography

We use the “Inter” font family to achieve clean, readable text with modern flair, pairing appropriate weights and sizes for hierarchy. Here, you’ll see how headings, body copy, and semantic elements should be formatted to maintain typographic consistency.

Title XL

  • font-family: Inter;

  • font-size: 1.5rem;

  • font-style: normal;

  • line-height: 1.2;

  • letter-spacing: -0.24px;

Title LG

  • font-family: Inter;

  • font-size: 1.25rem;

  • font-style: normal;

  • line-height: 1.2;

  • letter-spacing: -0.2px;

Title MD

  • font-family: Inter;

  • font-size: .8rem;

  • font-style: normal;

  • font-weight: 600;
